- Muiz: American Art in the Mountains
    - questions: Why do you like Bierstadt so much? Which of the pictures you
    showed us is your favorite?
    - he likes mountains and thus he combined the two things that he likes
    - _The belated Party on Mansfield Mountain_ (1858) -- really cool painting,
    Hudson River School
    - _A Gorge in the Mountains (Kauterskill Clove)_ (1862) -- nice meditative
    painting 
    - _Alaskan Coast Range_ (1889) -- great looking painting by his favorite
    painter
    - _A View of the Mountain Pass Called the Notch of the White Mountains
    (Crawford Notch)_ (1839) -- aftermath of an avalanche that killed
    a farmstead to show how small man is compared to nature
    - _Indian Horse in Mountains_ (1834) -- majestic scenery because of its
    simplicity
    - _A Storm in the Rocky Mountains, Mt. Rosalie_ (1866) -- named after his
    travelling companions wife, awesome picture with great play of light and
    shadow
